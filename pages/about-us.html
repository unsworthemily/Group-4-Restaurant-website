Our story

Restaurant Taste Buds is a newly opened Indian restaurant in Helsingborg city center. We are located in a very attractive location a stone's throw from the terrace steps at historic Kärnan. We serve traditional Indian food with fresh spices from India. Between 11:00 and 15:00 we serve lunch, which is already very popular. We also serve alcohol in our restaurant. We are open from 11:00 to 22:00 every day.
